3 Countries, 1 Degree

🇮🇹 Italy 🇫🇮 Finland 🇳🇱 Netherlands

My Erasmus Mundus MSc took me across 3 countries in two years. Which meant changing everything from phone numbers to addresses to friend circles in a few months. It was chaotic to say the least but I would do it all over again if given an opportunity. I got to see how people from completely different backgrounds approached the same problems and realized the answer was usually somewhere in between all of us.

Making Things With My Hands

I love all things crafty. Little experiments that don't always work out but I love the whole process. I have come to realize that painting, sewing, and sculpting is not that different from engineering; you start with a vague idea and figure it out as you go.

Hosting Workshops

I also love hosting crafting workshops centered around making art out of literal trash. People come together, make something with their hands and leave with something they are truly proud of.
